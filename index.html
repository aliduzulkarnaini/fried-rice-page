Fried Rice Recipe

Fried Rice is a quick and flavorful meal made with cooked rice, vegetables, eggs, and chicken, all stir-fried with soy sauce and simple seasonings. It is perfect for lunch or dinner and can be prepared in under 1 hour.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 1 hour
  • Preparation: 20 Minutes
  • Cooking: 40 Minutes
  • Servings: 4 People

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup cooked chicken (diced)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots and sweet corn)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper
  • Spring onions for garnish
  • Optional: Green pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large frying pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions and garlic. Stir-fry until soft and fragrant.
  3. Crack the eggs into the pan and scramble until fully cooked.
  4. Add the diced chicken and mixed vegetables. Cook for about three minutes while stirring continuously.
  5. Add the cooked rice and mix well so that the ingredients are evenly distributed.
  6. Pour in the soy sauce and season with salt and black pepper. Continue stirring until everything is coated.
  7. Cook for another three to five minutes until the fried rice is hot and slightly crispy.
  8. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve immediately.
